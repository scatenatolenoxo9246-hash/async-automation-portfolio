# Daily Execution Plan

Run this for 30 days.

## Daily 60-90 minute loop

1. Source 15 raw leads.
2. Score them using `icp_scorecard.csv`.
3. Keep the best 8-10.
4. Write a custom opening line for each kept lead.
5. Send 5-10 messages from the user's real account.
6. Log every sent message in `lead_tracker.csv`.
7. Follow up old leads due today.
8. Paste any replies to Codex for handling.

## Week 1: build the base

Targets:

- Complete user profile inputs.
- Publish or prepare portfolio link.
- Add 30 qualified leads.
- Send 15 personalized messages.
- Get first replies, even if negative.

## Week 2: test three customer pools

Targets:

- 20 Shopify agency leads
- 20 automation consultant leads
- 20 marketing/website agency leads
- 40 total outreach messages sent
- Track reply rate by segment

## Week 3: double down

Choose the best segment based on:

- Reply rate
- Quality of replies
- Fit with user's technical ability
- Payment likelihood

Targets:

- 50 new leads in best segment
- 50 outreach messages
- 3-5 meaningful replies
- 1-2 paid test proposals

## Week 4: convert and deliver

Targets:

- 1 paid test task
- 1 finished delivery
- 1 testimonial or anonymized case study
- Refine outreach based on real objections

## Metrics to track

Use these weekly:

- Raw leads found
- Qualified leads added
- Messages sent
- Replies
- Positive replies
- Discovery conversations
- Quotes sent
- Paid tasks
- Revenue
- Best-performing segment
- Most common objection

## Stop conditions

Stop or change the approach if:

- Reply rate is below 3% after 100 personalized messages.
- Replies are all free-work requests.
- Most leads are too large or irrelevant.
- The user cannot deliver the promised service.

## Success condition

Continue if:

- Reply rate is above 5%.
- At least one lead asks for examples, pricing, or scope.
- A paid test task seems plausible within 30 days.
